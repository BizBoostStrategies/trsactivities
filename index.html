2. What We Collect and Why

Website Analytics

What: Pages visited, time on site, general geographic region, scroll and click behavior.
How: Google Analytics 4 (GA4) with IP anonymization enabled by default.
Why: To understand how visitors use our site and improve our content.
What we do NOT collect: Names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information through analytics.

Free Sample Requests & Onboarding Forms

What: Name, email address, childcare center name, licensed capacity, and selected age groups. Forms are hosted by Microsoft Forms.
Why: To deliver your requested sample activities and, if you subscribe, to configure and deliver your daily activity emails.
Stored where: Secure Microsoft 365 Business cloud storage (OneDrive/Azure) with AES-256 encryption at rest. Data is not accessible to third parties.

Subscription & Payment Data

What: Subscription status, subscription ID, and the email address associated with your Stripe account.
How: Processed by Stripe. We do not store, access, or control your payment card data — Stripe is the data controller for all payment information. See stripe.com/privacy.
What we store: Only subscription status and your email, used solely to manage your active service.
